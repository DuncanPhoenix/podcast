Oxford's running community offers a mix of elite-led track sessions and inclusive social runs for 2026.

Weekly Group Training Schedule


Tuesday: Senior Training (Horspath Track)

Coach: Led by John Exley, a veteran "living legend" and Masters team manager for Oxford City Athletics Club (OCAC). Exley is a highly decorated athlete with a marathon personal best of 2:21. Focus: High-performance speed and endurance for seniors (18+) and veteran athletes of all abilities. Venue: Horspath Athletics & Sports Ground. Time: Track reserved for OCAC members from 5:30 pm to 8:00 pm.


Thursday: Distance & Stamina Improvement

Coach: Led by Neil Pinnell, focusing on building running volume and stamina for middle and long-distance improvement. Focus: Often involving interval training, these sessions are designed to increase lactic acid tolerance and develop running technique. Venue: Conducted at Horspath Track or various public parks and street-based locations depending on the season. Time: Typically starts around 6:00 pm to 7:00 pm.


Sunday: Social Long Runs

Focus: A "no-ego" social group of enthusiasts who run and chat, ideal for those needing a push to reach longer distances or looking to reconnect with the local running scene. Distance: Generally ranges from 10 to 16+ miles
