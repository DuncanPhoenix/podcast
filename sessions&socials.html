Oxrunners

Oxford's running community blends elite coaching with friendly, inclusive sessions for 2026.

Join runners who want stronger legs, smarter training, and a supportive local crew. Every session is designed to help you improve while enjoying the journey.

Weekly Group Training Schedule

Tuesday: Senior Training (Horspath Track)

Coach: John Exley, a veteran running leader and OCAC Masters manager. Focus: speed, endurance, and performance for committed runners. Venue: Horspath Athletics & Sports Ground. Time: 5:30 pm – 8:00 pm.


Thursday: Distance & Stamina Improvement

Coach: Neil Pinnell leads sessions that build sustainable mileage and improve race fitness. Focus: interval work, pacing, and technique. Venue: Horspath Track or local parks. Time: 6:00 pm – 7:00 pm.


Sunday: Social Long Runs

Focus: relaxed, no-ego group runs that help you build endurance with great company. Distance: typically 10–16 miles, with plenty of encouragement throughout.


Why Run With Us?

  • Expert-led sessions for all levels
  • Friendly community and team support
  • Practical advice on gear, recovery, and races
  • Local routes, events, and club connections

Ready to run faster, stay motivated, and feel part of a community? Oxford Runners is the place where every step counts.
